Our Creations

Handcrafted in Rome

Each piece in our collection is born from a dialogue between tradition and vision, shaped by hands that know gold and silver as intimately as words on a page. Explore our work below.

Rings

From solitaire engagement rings to elaborate cocktail pieces, each ring is sculpted to perfection — a circle of commitment or celebration cast in precious metal.

Necklaces & Pendants

Graceful chains and pendants that rest close to the heart — crafted to draw the eye and tell a story with every glimmer of light.

Earrings

Subtle studs or sweeping drops — our earrings frame the face with understated elegance or bold artistry, as the occasion demands.

Bracelets

Worn on the wrist, these pieces speak of refinement. Whether rigid bangles or fluid chain links, each bracelet is a small sculpture in its own right.

Brooches

A revived art form — our brooches are miniature masterpieces that bring life to lapels and scarves, blending historical inspiration with contemporary form.

Bespoke Creations

Your idea, our hands. We work closely with clients to bring unique visions to life — from initial sketch to the moment the finished piece is placed in your hands.
