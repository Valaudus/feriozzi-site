F.F. Feriozzi Orafi

Welcome to F.F. Feriozzi Orafi, where tradition, craftsmanship, and passion converge to create timeless treasures in the heart of Rome. As a cherished family legacy, our story is woven into the fabric of Rome's rich artisanal history.

At the helm of F.F. Feriozzi Orafi stands Fabio Feriozzi, the torchbearer of our family's heritage and a master craftsman in his own right. Every piece is crafted by hand, with an unwavering commitment to excellence and an unparalleled attention to detail.

Each creation bears the unmistakable mark of F.F. Feriozzi Orafi's artisanal mastery — a legacy forged in gold and silver, passed down through generations.
