Family tradition

At F.F. Feriozzi Orafi, our journey is deeply rooted in the rich history of Rome's artisanal tradition, where the art of jewelry-making has been revered for centuries. Today at the head of F.F. Feriozzi Orafi is Fabio Feriozzi, who from a tender age, found himself immersed in the mesmerizing world of craftsmanship under the watchful eye of his father, Franco.

Growing up surrounded by the tools of the trade and the creations taking shape in his father's atelier, Fabio developed a profound appreciation for the meticulous artistry that defined our family legacy. With each delicate movement of his father's skilled hands, a passion for the craft was ignited within Fabio — a passion that would become the driving force behind F.F. Feriozzi Orafi's continued success.

As Fabio honed his skills alongside his father, he absorbed not only the technical expertise passed down through generations but also the values of dedication and unwavering commitment to quality that defined their family's approach to jewelry-making.

Over the years, F.F. Feriozzi Orafi has emerged as one of the epicenters of handcrafted jewelry in Rome, celebrated for its ability to blend timeless aesthetics with modern sensibilities. Each piece that leaves the atelier is a testament to the family's storied heritage and the relentless pursuit of perfection that has been the hallmark of the Feriozzi name.

Our creations are more than mere adornments; they are heirlooms in the making, crafted to be passed down from generation to generation, carrying with them the essence of F.F. Feriozzi Orafi's unparalleled artisanal mastery and the eternal spirit of Rome.

Our Workshop

Nestled in the historic heart of Rome, our workshop on Via dell'Orso has been a place of creation for decades. Within these walls, gold and silver are transformed by skilled hands into wearable works of art.

We specialize in bespoke pieces — rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and brooches — each designed in close collaboration with our clients to ensure that every creation is as unique as the person who wears it.

Whether you seek a one-of-a-kind engagement ring, a meaningful gift, or a treasured keepsake, F.F. Feriozzi Orafi brings your vision to life with the finest materials and centuries of know-how.
